Chili

Description

The following recipe will show you how to create this delightful bowl of chili con carne.

Great for a cold winter evening, this dish will warm up not just your stomach but also your soul. Quick, simple, and tasty!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Canned tomatos
  • Kidney beans
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • White basmati rice
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, cayenne pepper powder, paprika

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef over a medium heat
  2. Add seasonings for flavour
  3. Toss in canned tomatos, crushing them with ladle
  4. Toss in kidney beans
  5. Stir and cover, allowing to simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Taste and add more seasonings if necessary
  7. If not thickened, allow to simmer for longer, stirring every few minutes until desired concistency is reached
  8. Boil rice, following intrusctions on packaging
  9. Add rice to bowl, ladle some chili con carne on top, topping with shredded cheese and a scoop of sour cream
