Lasagna

Description

The following recipe will teach you how to make this quick and tasty lasagna.

This is great when you don't want to stand over the stove for long periods of time or when you want to feed a party of people.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Shredded cheddar and mozzarella
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 Onion
  • Salt, pepper, and oregano
  • Canned tomatos and tomato puree
  • White lasagna sauce

Steps

  1. Brown onions over a medium heat along with the garlic
  2. Brown mince along with seasonings
  3. Add canned tomatos, crushing them with spoon or ladle
  4. Add tomato puree and mix
  5. Let simmer for 30 minutes - 1 hour
  6. Pre-boil pasta sheets if necessary
  7. Add lasagna sheets to dish, layered with meat sauce and white sauce
  8. Stack layers until meat and sauce are completely used, add a finishing sprinkle of cheese
  9. Bake in oven for 30-45 minutes
  10. Let cool for 5-10 minutes before serving
